I am a graduate student in Business Analytics at the University of Texas at Dallas. My research interests are machine learning related topics. Currently, I am working on reducing the impact of the filter bubble on the e-commerce and streaming recommendation systems. Prior to my academic career at UT Dallas, I studied Italian at Università Ca' Foscari Venezia in Italy. I graduated with a B.A. degree in Italian from Fu Jen Catholic University and with a M.S. degree in International Relations from National Sun Yat-sen University. After the completion of my degrees, I worked as a project manager with Taroko Door and Window Technologies Inc.. In addition to research, I am an easy-going person who loves cooking and traveling.
